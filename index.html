Credits

The first release of the game was created on the Construct 2 game engine in 2019 by studio IgrDsgn, featuring raster graphics.
In 2025, amidst the rise of neural networks, the idea emerged to rebuild the game with pure code and vector graphics.
Vector assets were sourced from www.freepik.com.
Special thanks to the authors: @grmarc, @brgfx, and @freepik.
Neural networks involved: Deepseek, Qwen, Claude, and Krea.
A special shoutout to our main hero, Dimasik!
Unfortunately, generating a face that truly resembles our beloved character proved challenging.
We're eagerly awaiting future upgrades to bring Dimasik to life even more authentically.
Stay tuned!
